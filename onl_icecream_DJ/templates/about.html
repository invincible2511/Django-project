Acronto Ice Creams and Products

It’ll blow your mind.

In the world of Acronto Ice Cream, happiness and excitement go hand-in-hand with good health and delicious flavours. Made from Fresh Milk (Real Milk, Real Ice cream): No added vegetable oil, hence no Frozen Dessert. No Differentiation in Quality Standards of Consumer & Catering/ Institutional Packs: Stringent Standards than Specified by PFA & BIS for more fat and less overrun (less air) to give creamier feeling.
